## What this measures, and what it does not

This eval answers one question: does installing the chiron kit move doctrine-targeted behaviors on a given model, and at what cost. It does NOT show that a weaker model becomes a stronger one. Single-prompt headless runs test doctrine content, not long-session delivery. Tasks and checks were written by the doctrine's author and were committed before the first scored run (pre-registration); overfitting to the task genre remains a real limitation. Scores are fractions of pre-registered mechanical sub-checks; no LLM judging.
